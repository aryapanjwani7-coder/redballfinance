How the Book is Kept

The Method

  • Paper portfolio No real money is moved — it's built for learning and accountability.
  • Net Asset Value Total value in rupees: marked-to-market holdings plus dividends received, starting from ₹1 crore and benchmarked against the Nifty 50.
  • Stock pages Each holding has a rupee price chart, a live position snapshot, and a written report.
  • Data Prices, dividends and the Nifty 50 are fetched daily after the close; the buys live in transactions.json.
