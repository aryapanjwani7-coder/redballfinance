About

Red Ball Finance is a transparent, educational paper portfolio. Every position has a public thesis, and performance is tracked daily using market-close data.

How the site works

  • Paper portfolio: No real money is moved. It’s built for learning and accountability.
  • NAV: Shows total value in USD (cash + marked-to-market holdings), indexed to US$ 10,000,000 at inception.
  • Stock pages: Each page includes the local-currency price chart and a written report.
  • Data: Prices are fetched from a Google Sheet; buys are defined in data/transactions.json.
